Volunteer Work

Jasmine has been volunteering for many years with Friends of Children with Special Needs (FCSN), a Bay Area nonprofit dedicated to enriching the lives of children and young adults with developmental disabilities. As a volunteer instructor, she teaches dance, music, and other creative activities to kids with special needs — bringing joy, confidence, and a love of movement and art into their lives.
